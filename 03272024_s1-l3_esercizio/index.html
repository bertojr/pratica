Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste bods with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with seet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creany brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cramberry Recipes That Go Beyon Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarn water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrabe rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I someimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour
